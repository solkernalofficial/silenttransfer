## Video direction

- **palette system:** ink `#0B1220` canvas/ground · cream/white type on dark frames · accent green `#15803D` / deep `#14532D` for seals, underlines, CTA · soft green `#ECFDF5` only on light cards · monochrome editorial with one green voltage (from frame.md roles: ink/cream/pink-as-green)
- **motion grammar + reveal model:** long-tail `power3` settles · VO-paced reveals only (never dump at t=0) · kinetic hard-cut word swaps and staggered card assemble · subtle jitter on final holds only · no bouncy overshoot
- **rhythm / held-frame allocation:** Frame 1–2 tense and fast · Frame 3 held brand resolve · Frame 4–5 mechanism clarity · Frame 6 still CTA lockup (breather)
- **negative list:** no purple AI gradients · no browser chrome · no fake anonymity claims · no slideshow front-load · no screensaver floating cards · caption keep-out bottom 17%
